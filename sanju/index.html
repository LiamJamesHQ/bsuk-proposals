02 — The concept

Your build, as we're picturing it

A floating, wood-fired commercial sauna, designed around compliance, privacy, and the long game.

The elements
  • FormGlass-fronted, straight-edge sauna on a floating pontoon. Clean and modern, making the most of a lakeside outlook.
  • HeatWood-burning, with the log feed running through the changing room so wood is loaded without stepping into the heat.
  • ChangingTwo changing rooms, one at each end, behind frosted heat-trap doors. Men's and women's, kept private.
  • PorchA covered porch running the front of the deck, a considered addition that sets your build apart and handles the British weather.
  • LightingWarm, low ambient glow. Under-seat and behind the backrest only, no ceiling spotlights. Privacy first.
  • ControlsWi-Fi heater on a schedule and online booking with waiver built in, low-maintenance to run day to day.

05 — The process

From deposit to delivery

A clear run of what happens, and when, so you can plan your side around it.

  • Deposit & conceptOnce your deposit's in, your concept drawings land within a week. We then tweak until it's perfect.
  • Sign-off & orderWhen the design's right and you're ready, your build goes into the queue.
  • Build & lead timeCurrent build lead time is around 7–8 weeks. Allowing for scheduling and delivery, plan for roughly three months order-to-delivery, a little more for the pontoon.

If plans change

If the council comes back

You asked what happens if planning or a regulatory body comes back with changes. That's exactly what the concept stage is for. While you're refining the design with us, revisions are unlimited. If the council or a certifying body asks for an adjustment, we rework the concept with you until it's approved and right.

Price excludes delivery and certification, which vary by mooring location and are arranged separately.
