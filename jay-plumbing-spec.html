Drainage & water — first-fix before delivery

Prepared for Jay · Barrel Sauna UK — Sauna, steam room & hot tub fit-out

Get this in before the units arrive. Once the sauna, steam room and hot tub are set in place, the channels behind them can't be cut. First-fixing all of the below now, while the walls are open, saves reopening anything later.

This is everything your plumber needs to channel out and first-fix before the units land. Positions for the sauna and steam room are already agreed. Anything marked to confirm is a quick on-site check with us before the channels close, not a hold-up — your trades can run everything else now.

At a glance

  • 2 × 100mm (4") drainage runs out the rear wall behind the sauna & steam units, to gutter or external drain
  • One concealed water tap near the units for filling — no plumbed feed into the barrels needed
  • Hot tub: fill from the concealed tap; one drain-down point or soakaway to empty
  • All runs laid to fall so they clear on their own

One

Drainage — sauna & steam room

Pipe & size
2 × 100mm (4") drainage pipes, one behind each unit, exiting straight through the rear wall.
Discharge
Out to a gutter or external drain — your call on which is nearest and cleanest to reach.
Fall / gradient
Lay each run to a steady fall so water clears without pooling. A standard drainage fall (around 1:40) is ample.
Final position
The steam unit's drain point wants a quick sign-off from us before you cut the channel — it sits to a set spot on the base.Confirm exact position with BSUK before walls close

The two-pipe layout you suggested is spot on — this just pins the size and fall so your plumber can run them now, with the one steam-drain position checked on the day the base goes in.

Two

Water supply — filling the units

Feed needed
No plumbed-in feed into the sauna or steam barrels. They don't take a permanent water line.
Fill method
One concealed tap sited near the units covers all filling — exactly as you suggested. Tuck it out of sight but within easy reach of a hose or bucket.
Position
Wherever suits your first-fix and stays hidden from the finished room. No fixed spot required from our side.

Three

Hot tub — fill & empty

Fill
From the same concealed tap — hose or bucket fill, as you're happy to do yourself. No dedicated feed to the tub needed.
Empty
One drain-down point or soakaway within reach of the tub so it can be emptied down when needed. Size it to a standard garden drain — nothing heavy-duty.
Method
A simple concealed-tap fill and a drain point to empty is all it needs.Confirm tub position & drain reach with BSUK on the day

Kept deliberately simple — fill from the hidden tap, empty to a drain point. That matches how you said you'd rather handle it, and saves plumbing a permanent line into the tub.
